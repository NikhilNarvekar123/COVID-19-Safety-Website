Corona In Your Area

Are you at risk?

Using GPS data, our application tracks your location and finds out whether you have been in contact with a COVID-19 patient. It also shows the location of reported cases in your area. Be aware that this application is by no means an official test for the Corona-virus. You may still have the virus without coming into contact with others. Please consult a doctor for any concerns.
